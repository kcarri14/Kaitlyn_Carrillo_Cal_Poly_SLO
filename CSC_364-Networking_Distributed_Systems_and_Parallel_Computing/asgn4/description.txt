Once the peers are started they send a message to the server that
includes their name and port. The server stores this information and 
sends back the table to the peer. The peer will then look at the table and
send offers to all other peers that are in the table excluding themselves. 
The other peers will send requests and the primary peer will transfer all the 
of the files that the other peers requested. Once the other peers receive the files,
they will send an acknowledgement to the primary peer. The other peers will also send offers 
to the primary peer for the files that they have and do the whole process again. 
In the end, all of the peers will have all of the files that each other has.
